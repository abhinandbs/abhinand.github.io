RoadVigil Android App | Reduce vehicle traffic during corona lock down time.

Roadvigil released the beta version within in 3 days. It was used all over kerala by kerala police to control crowds during the covid pandemic in 2019. It was released by Kerala-DGP Lokanath Behera. It runs successfully without any issues till the lockdown ends. (Helps to Reduce corona count in Kerala) It had about 1 lakh+ traffic daily.
